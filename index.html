Gyaxo

What is Gyaxo?

Gyaxo is a song library filled with all the top 100 songs from each month from 1990-present day. The songs are drawn from the top 100 billboards API based on the month you chose. Gyaxo also has the capibility to stream music straight to your computer from your selected month you chose.

Gyaxo Features

  • Skip
  • Select specific songs
  • Select a specific time within the song
  • See the number of listens

    • How to use Gyaxo

    To use Gyaxo simply put in the ["Year,Month"] into the search box.
